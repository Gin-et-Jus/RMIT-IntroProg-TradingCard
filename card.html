Grey Wolf

The Alpha dog, leading the pack to victory

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Length: 160cm
  • Average Lifespan: 16 years
  • Habitats: Arctic Tundra, Forest, Prairie

A social and cooperative predator, specialized for game hunting with highly advanced expressive behavior. Respected in some hunter-gatherer societies and feared in pastoral societies due to extended interactions with humans, this animal is highly territorial and will often fight other packs.
